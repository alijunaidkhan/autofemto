Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to AutoFemto ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you visit our website at autofemto.com.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information through Google Analytics, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Device information

2.2 Tool Usage Data

Important: All our tools process data entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter into our tools (text, images, JSON data, etc.). Your data never leaves your device.

2.3 Contact Form Information

If you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content
  • Service type and budget (optional)

This information is processed by Formspree and used solely to respond to your inquiry.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Analyze website traffic and improve user experience
  • Understand which tools are most popular
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Ensure website security and prevent abuse

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

4.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4.2 Google AdSense (When Implemented)

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4.3 Essential Cookies

Some of our tools may use local storage in your browser to save your preferences (e.g., undo history in Text Tools). This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Google Analytics: For website traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements (when implemented)
  • Formspree: For processing contact form submissions
  • GitHub Pages: Our hosting provider

These third parties have their own privacy policies and may use cookies and tracking technologies.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all pages
  • Client-side processing for tool data (nothing stored on servers)
  • Secure hosting through GitHub Pages
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Opt-out: Opt out of cookies and tracking

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By using our website, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

13. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Our legal basis for processing your data is consent (for cookies and analytics) and legitimate interests (for website operation and improvement).

14. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
