Frequently Asked Questions

Why are there two types of gallons (US and UK)?

Historical reasons. US gallon = 3.785 liters, UK gallon = 4.546 liters. Always specify which one.

What's the difference between mass and weight?

Mass: Amount of matter (kg, grams). Constant everywhere.
Weight: Force due to gravity (newtons, pounds-force). Varies by location.

In everyday use, "weight" usually means mass.

Why do computers use 1024 instead of 1000?

Computers use binary (base-2). 210 = 1,024, which is close to 1,000, so 1 KB = 1,024 bytes (historically).

Are all unit conversions exact?

Most are defined exactly (1 inch = exactly 2.54 cm). Some, like currency exchange rates, change constantly.
